PEACOCKS TAKE OVER CAMPUS???

Three peacocks randomly strutted into campus like they paid tuition. One chased anyone who moved too fast. Another stared at its reflection in a vending machine for ages. Security just stared back. If someone owns these birds… why.

CHRISTMAS FAIR IS COMING (pray for us)

The Christmas Fair is happening soon and nobody is ready. Rumours say: a giant inflatable Santa that might fall over, a bake sale run by people who burn toast, hot chocolate so strong it might be illegal, and way too many fairy lights. Place your bets on what breaks first.

CAMPUS LIFE

POST-IT NOTES EVERYWHERE

Someone left tiny motivational notes all over campus. Things like “U got this” and “Drink water pls.” People keep finding them in weird places and smiling like idiots. It’s mysterious and slightly wholesome. Keep the notes coming.

OPINION

9AM CLASSES SHOULD BE ILLEGAL

Nobody’s brain works at 9AM. Move everything later. Life would improve. Attendance would rise. Also coffee would be less dramatic. That is the argument. Fight me.
